EDS 137

Visual Arts Education

WHO

This class focuses on K-12 students involved in VAPA (Visual and Performing Arts)

WHAT

Some of the topics covered in this class are:

  • VAPA in relation to the education systems in the U.S. and the academic achievement of students
  • the structure of schools and their roles in the community
  • the impact of public service on individuals and the community

    • COOL POINTS

  • Have the opportunity to serve as a mentor to support a child's academic growth through meaningful VAPA experience
  • Fulfills DEI requirement
