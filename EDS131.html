EDS 131

Early Childhood Education

WHO

This class focuses on education for young children, aged 2-5 years old

WHAT

The topics covered in this class are:

  • the place of public service in U.S. society
  • the social and political organization of schools
  • the philosophical, sociological, and political issues which relate to education
  • the academic achievemnt of children

    • COOL POINTS

  • Get to spend 4-6 hours a week acting as tutors, mentors, and/or facilitators in classrooms, labs, and after school programs at a number of low-income neighborhood schools throughout San Diego County
